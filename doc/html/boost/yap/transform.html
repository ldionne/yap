Function template transform

boost::yap::transform

Synopsis

// In header: <boost/yap/expression.hpp>


template<typename Expr, typename Transform> 
  decltype(auto) transform(Expr && expr, Transform && transform);

Description

Returns the result of transforming (all or part of) expr using whatever overloads of Transform::operator() that match expr.

Note

Transformations can do anything: they may have side effects; they may mutate values; they may mutate types; and they may do any combination of these.
